How Search Works:
	Use software called crawlers, index all the words on a page, and follows all links on the page
	First search result is the page that contains the most links to pages that contain terms you are searching for
	Google strips unnecessary words out of search

Commands and Colons:
	Limits searches to a particular using the site: keyword
	To a filetype using filetype:
	Clever searches that reveal things that are not meant to be indexed are called Google Dorks

Wildcards and Quotes:
	* is a wildcard operator that matches any word
	Quotes only searches for an exact term
